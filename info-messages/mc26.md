everyone: true
---

As of Minecraft 26.1 dimensions are no longer in `DIM1` and `DIM-1` on vanilla/modded, or `world_nether` and `world_the_end` on Spigot/Paper based servers.
**BlueMap will not automatically migrate your map configs.**
You have to manually change configs such as
```yml
world: "world_nether"
dimension: "minecraft:the_nether"
```
to
```yml
world: "world"
dimension: "minecraft:the_nether"
```
BlueMap will figure out the actual region path from the dimension key.
Manually pointing to the internal dimension folder will work by chance but is _not recommended_.
